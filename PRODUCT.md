# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Customers scanning a QR code at their table inside Andi's Coffee — students from the nearby University of Jordan, professionals, and specialty coffee lovers in Jubeiha, Amman. They're on mobile, on cafe Wi-Fi or 3G/4G, browsing the menu to decide what to order. Speed matters: the page must load before their drink arrives.

Secondary: Instagram visitors who find the cafe through @andis_coffee and land on a link-in-bio page. They want menu highlights, location, and a feel for the place — fast.

## Product Purpose

A lightweight, instantly-loading digital menu that replaces slow web apps and PDF menus. Success means a customer scans the QR, sees the full menu with prices in under 2 seconds, and orders without friction. No app install, no heavy JavaScript, no tapping to reveal prices.

The broader goal: package this as a sellable product for other cafes in Amman who need the same thing.

## Brand Personality

Warm, earthy, refined. The brand green (#4C5A3E) is deliberate and unapologetic. The design feels like a well-kept cafe menu — tactile, clear, proud of its craft. Arabic and English sit side by side naturally. Premium but never cold or corporate.

## Anti-references

- Heavy React SPAs that blank out on slow networks
- PDF menus that require pinch-zooming on a phone
- Cluttered fast-food menu designs
- Generic AI-generated cafe templates (beige-on-beige, Fraunces font, glassmorphism)
- Any design where prices are hidden behind interaction

## Design Principles

1. **Load is the feature** — The menu must render instantly on Jordanian 3G/4G. Every byte matters. No JS frameworks, no external dependencies beyond web fonts.
2. **Prices first** — Customers scan menus by price. Every item shows its price immediately. No taps to reveal.
3. **Mobile-native** — Designed for thumb-friendly scrolling on a phone screen. Desktop is secondary.
4. **Bilingual by default** — English and Arabic are equals. The toggle is instant with no page reload.
5. **On-brand without the weight** — Uses Andi's existing brand identity (green #4C5A3E, Cormorant Garamond + Alexandria) but stripped down to essentials. No decorative cruft.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA minimum. Body text contrast ≥4.5:1 against background. Respect reduced motion preferences. Touch targets ≥44px. Semantic HTML for screen readers.
